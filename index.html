Chapter 1: Meet the Pigs

The Beginning

Once upon a time, there were three little pigs who lived with their mother. One day, she told them it was time to go out into the world and build homes of their own.

Each pig had a different idea of what kind of house to build. But none of them knew that a big, bad wolf was watching them from the woods, waiting for his chance.
