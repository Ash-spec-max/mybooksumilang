Chapter 2: The Straw House

Straw House

The first little pig quickly built a house out of straw. It was fast and easy, and he had plenty of time to play afterward.

But the big bad wolf came and said, “Little pig, little pig, let me come in!”

“Not by the hair on my chinny chin chin!” cried the pig. The wolf huffed and puffed—and blew the straw house down!

The first pig ran to his brother’s house in fear.
