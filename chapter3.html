Chapter 3: The Wooden House

Wooden House

The second little pig had built his house out of sticks. It was stronger than straw, but still not very sturdy.

The two pigs hid inside. The wolf appeared again and growled, “Little pigs, little pigs, let me come in!”

“Not by the hair on our chinny chin chins!” they shouted. So the wolf huffed and puffed—and blew the stick house down!

Terrified, the two pigs ran to their brother’s brick house.
