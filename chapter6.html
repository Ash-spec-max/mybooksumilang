The Three Little Pigs - Chapter Summary

Chapter 1: Meet the Pigs

Three pigs leave home to build their own houses—one lazy, one playful, and one wise.

Chapter 2: The Straw House

The first pig builds a straw house, but it’s quickly blown down by the big bad wolf.

Chapter 3: The Wooden House

The second pig uses sticks, but his house is also destroyed by the wolf’s powerful breath.

Chapter 4: The Brick House

The third pig builds a strong brick house. The wolf tries to blow it down, but fails.

Chapter 5: The Wolf’s End

The wolf sneaks down the chimney—but lands in boiling water and flees for good.
